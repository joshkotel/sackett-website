A Bit About Us...

Our history begins in 2008, with a simple mission to provide a cozy local pub to unwind for a while while enjoying a great drink, and connecting with friends.

You can also join us for trivia every Tuesday at 8pm – no cover charge and always free to play! Stay tuned to our socials for updates on upcoming trivia, themed nights, and special happenings.

Our happy hour is every day 4pm - 7pm
