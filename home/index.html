A Bit About Us...

The Sackett first opened back in 2008, right on the border of Park Slope and Gowanus. The mission has always been to create a warm and inviting environment for everyone to enjoy - to come unwind with a great drink and connect with friends. Our house is your house.

You are also invited to join our Trivia Night every Tuesday at 8pm – no cover charge and always free to play! Stay tuned to our socials for updates on upcoming trivia, themed nights, and special happenings.

Our happy hour is every day 4pm - 7pm
